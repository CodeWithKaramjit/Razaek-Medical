Step-by-Step Care

How It Works

From your initial medical file submission to your departure back home, we handle all administrative, medical, and hospitality logistics.

Step 1

Free Medical Case Review

Submit your medical reports and history. Our panel of senior specialists in India reviews your diagnostic records within 48 hours to confirm treatment feasibility.

Step 2

Custom Medical Travel Quote

Receive a transparent treatment and travel itinerary estimate covering surgery costs, hospital stay, flights, luxury recuperation lodging, and visa requirements.

Step 3

VIP Concierge Welcome

Touch down in India. Your private companion coordinates your VIP airport pick-up, local SIM card, luxury transport, and check-in to your recuperation accommodation.

Step 4

Hospital Admission & Treatment

Meet your lead surgeon for final pre-op diagnostics. Your treatment takes place in leading JCI-accredited sterile operating theatres with 24/7 dedicated support.

Step 5

Recuperation & Local Tourism

Recover in tranquility. We coordinate nurse visits, physiotherapy, wellness meals, and guided light tourism excursions to experience the culture and beauty of India.

Step 6

Departure & Telehealth Follow-up

Fly back home with certified medical discharge files and prescribed medications. Benefit from post-treatment telehealth check-ins with your surgical team.
